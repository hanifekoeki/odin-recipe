Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until soft and translucent. Stir in the garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until browned, breaking it up with a wooden spoon as it cooks.
  4. Stir in the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened.
  5. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the Bolognese sauce. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Enjoy your delicious, homemade spaghetti Bolognese !
