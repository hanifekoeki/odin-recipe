Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
  2. In a separate bowl, sift together cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  3. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture. Stir until just combined; avoid overmixing.
  4. Preheat your oven to 350°F. Grease and line an 8x8-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips for extra richness.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread it evenly. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

Let the brownies cool completely before cutting into squares. And if you like you can top it with vanilla ice cream. Enjoy!🍫
